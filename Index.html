About the System

This web-based integrated management platform is designed to improve the operations of lying-in clinics in Cavite by digitizing patient records, monitoring maternal health, and providing decision support to healthcare professionals. The system also includes a mobile application for convenient access and real-time updates.

Core Features

Patient Management

Manage prenatal records, patient profiles, and medical history efficiently.

Decision Support System

Provides risk assessment and recommendations based on maternal data.

Clinic Reports

Generate reports for deliveries, prenatal visits, and clinic performance.

Mobile Application

Access patient data, alerts, and notifications through a mobile device.

Target Users

  • Clinic Administrators
  • Midwives and Doctors
  • Clinic Staff
